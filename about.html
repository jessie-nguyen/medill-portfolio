Hello! My name is Jessie.

I'm currently a M.Sc. candidate at Northwestern University, pursuing a specialization in investigative journalism in the Medill Investigative Labs and a concentration in data journalism. I'm also a marketing communications specialist with The Democracy Group where I produce content that emphasizes civic engagement, civil discourse and democracy.

I graduated from Penn State University in 2023 with a B.A. in broadcast journalism, two minors in economics and business & the liberal arts, and a diversity studies certificate. At Penn State, I covered local news, student life, arts & culture and current events for Penn State's student-run publication The Daily Collegian and Penn State's newscast Centre County Report.

You can find my bylines on Capitol News Illinois, LancasterOnline, NBCU Local, WNEP.com, CapeCodXplore.com and Penn State News.

Please feel free to get in touch with me via email or LinkedIn! Happy reading!
