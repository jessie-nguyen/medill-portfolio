Hello!

Jessie Nguyen is a graduate student at Northwestern University, where she specializes in the Medill Investigative Lab with a concentration in data journalism. She graduated from Penn State University in 2023 with a degree in Broadcast Journalism and two minors in Economics and Business. At Penn State, Jessie wrote for Penn State's student-run publication The Daily Collegian and anchored Penn State's newscast Centre County Report.

Before coming to Medill, Jessie reported for LancasterOnline, covering school budgets, K-12 education and community news in southeastern Pennsylvania. At Medill, Jessie has covered the statewide teacher shortage for Capitol News Illinois, Appalachian coal minersâ€™ fight for benefits for the Pittsburgh Post-Gazette, and the FDA's oversight of generic drug manufacturing for ProPublica.

Jessie is also a marketing communications specialist with The Democracy Group where she produces content that emphasizes civic engagement, civil discourse and democracy building. She has worked for NBCUniversal Local, WNEP-TV, Centre County Report and The Daily Collegian. Jessie has a passion for local news reporting and a strong interest in policies, economics and education.
